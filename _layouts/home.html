Colophon

This site is written by hand in Sublime Text, assembled with Jekyll, and hosted on Github. Headings are set in Geom, a novel geometric typeface designed by Thanos Poulakidas in 2022, and the remaining text is set in Junicode, originally digitized by medievalist Peter Baker in the 1990s from type designed by Franciscus Junius in the 17th century.
